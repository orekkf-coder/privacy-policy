Privacy Policy（隐私政策）

1. Data Collection（数据收集）

Our app collects the following data:

  • Apple Sign-In Identifier: Used to identify users.
  • User-Provided Information: Name, email, phone number (optional).
  • Business Data: Asset information and profile records.

2. Data Storage（数据存储）

All data is stored locally on your device with AES-256 encryption. No data is transmitted to external servers.

3. Third-Party Services（第三方服务）

Our app does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

4. User Rights（用户权利）

You can export, delete, or reset your data at any time.
